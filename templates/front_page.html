Use guide

But wait! How do I use this nifty platform? Well, it's actually quite simple by following these steps:

  1. Login/Register an account with a valid email and password
  2. You will be taken to your dashboard which has a button on the top to start a new interview.
  3. When you start an interview you will be taken to the set up page, where you can select interview length (5 questions or 10 questions), interview type (general, sales, cs, it), and can adjust yourself so that your webcam is positioned on you properly.
  4. During the interview you can send responses that capture eye contact by pressing the speak button, which captures both eye contact through recording your webcam, and using speech to text to capture your speech (which is editable in the text window) simultaneously.
  5. Pressing the send button sends your message, and your webcam recording for processing, which means it will take some time for the next question to be sent from the bot.
  6. Once the interview is finished you will be redirected to your dashboard once more and the latest interview session will be at the top left of the dashboard.
  7. Clicking on that will give you your responses and the feedback of each response in an easy-to-read format, as well as your eye contact level during the interview! Isn't that cool?

With that you should be easily able to prepare for your dream job with zero hassle! Click on the button to start with your AI interview now!
